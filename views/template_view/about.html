About Us:

Welcome to ISTIC-LIBRARY! Our website is designed to provide a platform for ISTIC University students to upload and access useful resources for studying. At ISTIC-LIBRARY, we believe that students learn best when they have access to a variety of high-quality resources. That's why we created this site, to make it easy for students to share their own materials and benefit from the knowledge and experience of their peers.


Here are some of the key features of ISTIC-LIBRARY:

  • Upload and download files: Users can easily upload files in a variety of formats, including documents, images, and videos. They can also search for and download files uploaded by other users.

  • Search for resources: Our site has a powerful search feature that allows users to find resources quickly and easily. They can search by keyword, file type, or other criteria.
